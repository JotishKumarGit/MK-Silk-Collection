About Us

Welcome to M.K. silk collection, your trusted partner in premium Banarasi silk textiles. Established in 2020 in the heart of Varanasi, we bring you the finest collection of traditional and contemporary Banarasi silk sarees.

Under the leadership of Mr. Kursid Alam, our dedicated team of 5 skilled professionals works tirelessly to manufacture, export, and supply the highest quality silk products. With 5 years of expertise in the industry, we have built a reputation for excellence and authenticity.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us achieve an annual turnover of ₹20,00,000, serving customers across India and beyond.
